# Concurrency

Concurrency focuses on the design and implementation of systems that can execute multiple tasks simultaneously.

## Topics

- Transition Systems
- Parallelism
- Channel Systems
- Handshaking
- NanoPromela
- ILP
- DLP
- TLP
- Data Coherence
- Synchronization Primitives
- Processes and Threads
